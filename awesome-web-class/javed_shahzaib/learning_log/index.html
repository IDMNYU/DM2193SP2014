Reading Response

Your design should tell a story. It should help guide the reader rather than confuse or misguide the user. For instance, changing the size and color of a particlar website may change the way the user interacts with the site or even persuay them to leave. On the other hand, some combinations will encourage the user and have them return more likely. The design also plays a role in how the words or which words are emphasized. In essence, your design should tell a story and that story will play a key factor in guiding the user.
