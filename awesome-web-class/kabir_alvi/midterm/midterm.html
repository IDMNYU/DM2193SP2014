Home Page

My wireframes+designs offer more functional views than the current Craigslist. It has a login on the home page and condenses most of the content under general categories. There is a chat box added that allows Craigslist users to interact with one another via chat. In the search view, the results are listed by a given interval. Pagination at the bottom of the page allows browsing through different pages of results. The filter box allows you to narrow down your results to results that adhere to the criteria you select. In the profile page, you can see what listings you have posted, and choose to search/filter/delete them.
