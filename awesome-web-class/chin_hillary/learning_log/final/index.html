Web Edition
Play

This is a decision-based narrative web adaptation of the episodic interactive video game, Life is Strange. This website depicts the story with images, captions, thoughts, and dialogue. Navigate through the site by scrolling down, pressing the down arrow key, or making a decision when prompted by the scene in order to progress the story. The story line is non-linear, so the outcome and your relationships with other characters are determined by the choices you make.
