reading response

five easy ways to be a better web professional was an interesting blog, because it presents web design as something more established than people like to credit it. even though the web is still very new, there are many standards and practices in place that you have to acknowledge when you begin web design, and it's easy to forget these and make some messy, mediocre websites. of particular importance is respecting the audience; it's easy to get caught up in your own creation and focus on why you love it so much, but when the audience doesn't approve, it needs to be changed, no matter what you think.
