Reading Response
PROGRAMMING IS NOT A CRAFT

My Response

Reading the title the way it was presented in the syllabus really touched me the wrong way. Seeing the word not associated with the craftmanship angered me. How could it not be a craft? But when I clicked the article to read more, and read the title then I understood. I agree wholeheartedly, 100% with the contents of that article. As a matter of fact, about a semester ago I was speaking with a friend of mine about my interest in software programming. When I was talking with her, I explained it in such a way that she had to comment and say "it sounds so much like your describing at art piece." It was at that moment that I began to think of it as such.
