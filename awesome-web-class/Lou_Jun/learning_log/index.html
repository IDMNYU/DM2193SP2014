    PROGRAMMING IS NOT A CRAFT

    While many can believe what they want to believe, many don't understand the true values behind certain jobs. Is programming a craft? To people who are not familiar with it, probably not. There are many factors that a programmer thinks of that is similar to that of an artist. The programmer is both a designer and developer at one point. As such they have hold on to their own values and principles in the craft they create. Which is what makes me believe that programming is a craft. Besides it is quite tedious like many other so call "crafts."
